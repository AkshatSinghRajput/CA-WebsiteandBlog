BIG ENOUGH TO SPECIALIZE, SMALL ENOUGH TO PERSONALIZE

The Partners of Chopda and Kataria came together in 2011.

While we have grown over the years, we have retained our small firm values, of family and trust.

What makes us different is the entrepreneurial spirit of our team, our commitment and dedication towards servicing our clients and our passion for constantly upgrading our technical expertise.

Each member of the team remains dedicated to creating a dynamic and collaborative workspace while staying curious, driven, and inspired. Our team has professionals with advanced degrees and many of them enroute to achieving additional academic honours.

WE ARE OPEN TO CHANGE,
BUT FIRM ON OUR VALUES

Deliver Results

We value your time and ensure promises are kept. We implement controls to deliver on time and assuring the best quality of service.

Find a better way

A culture of innovation to seek a smarter and better work lifestyle. We integrate latest and emerging technology into our work by use of various digital means such as online accounting, office management software, cloud storage, remote working capabilities etc, to foster persistent professional growth.

Continuously Improve

We constantly push ourselves to be our best, we focus on solutions, and try to make a difference everyday through our talents, passion and hard work.

We Think Long Term

Do The Right Thing

Honesty is first and foremost for us. We uphold our integrity and work ethics intact while serving our clients and community
