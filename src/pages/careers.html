JOIN US TO
MAKE A DIFFERNCE

We at Chopda and Kataria create a respectful work environment that has a great work life balance. A dynamic office where we thrive together in the spirit of collaboration and fellowship. We work hard to accomplish our targets on time. After that, we celebrate.

Our team members and associates have easy access to partners through an active mentorship program. One on one meetings are scheduled to discuss career and personal goals. Moreover, we offer ongoing career development program for associates where assignments are shuffled to meet interests as well as opportunities for continuing education.

WHO WE LOOK FOR

We always are on the look-out for the inquisitive, hard-working, and committed. We believe in the “Learn by Doing” axiom and are looking for new associates who are open- minded and curious. We are looking for associates who take the initiative to learn new things and are forward-looking.

Chopda & Kataria is a family, and as such, we are looking for people comfortable working in a collaborative environment.
